## Understanding AdSense earnings
AdSense earnings for Indian YouTubers vary widely depending on their niche, audience engagement, and RPM. Here are some estimated AdSense earnings for different niches:
1. **Gaming**: ₹200 - ₹500 per 1,000 views
2. **Vlogging**: ₹150 - ₹300 per 1,000 views
3. **Beauty and fashion**: ₹100 - ₹250 per 1,000 views
4. **Cooking**: ₹80 - ₹200 per 1,000 views
5. **Education**: ₹50 - ₹150 per 1,000 views

To give you a better understanding of how AdSense earnings work, let's break down the process:
1. **Ad placement**: YouTubers place ads on their videos, either manually or through AdSense.
2. **Ad views**: Viewers watch the ads, generating revenue for the YouTuber.
3. **RPM calculation**: AdSense calculates the RPM based on the number of ad views, clicks, and other factors.
4. **Earnings distribution**: AdSense distributes the earnings to the YouTuber, typically on a monthly basis.

For instance, let's say a YouTuber has a RPM of ₹250 and 10,000 views per month. Their estimated AdSense earnings would be ₹2,500 per month. Another example is a YouTuber with a RPM of ₹500 and 50,000 views per month, earning ₹25,000 per month from AdSense.
